WHAT IS IEDC ?

Why IEDC ?

The Innovation and Entrepreneurship Development Centres (IEDC) are platforms set up by the KSUM (Kerala Start-Up Mission) in colleges and institutions with an aim to provide students with an opportunity to experiment and innovate their ideas. IEDC's work as the first launchpad needed for a students entrepreneurial journey allowing them to collaborate and transform their innovative ideas into services and products. It plays an essential role in the fostering of experimenting and innovation culture in academic institutions.
